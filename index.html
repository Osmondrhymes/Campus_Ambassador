Join Grazac's Campus Ambassador Community!

Join a community of influential student innovators, creatives, and techies dedicated to transforming their campus and championing Grazac’s mission in their respective institutions.

What's in it for you?

Exclusive Access/Tickets to Grazac Events

Each year, Grazac hosts standout events like the Ogun Digital Summit (ODS) across Ogun State. As a Campus Ambassador, enjoy exclusive tickets to events!

Access to Career Resources

Being a Campus Ambassador means having unlimited access to career mentorship from Grazac's professionals, financial support for events, monthly data allowance, and exclusive perks such as free co-working space.

Exposure & Hands-on Experience

Gain personal and professional growth as Campus Ambassador through access to industrial training, internships, and potential job interviews upon graduation/postgraduate.

Spotlight and Showcase

Showcase your journey as a campus ambassador on our website and share your story with the world!
